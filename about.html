Hi I'm Gabriella Corales

Front-End Developer, based in the U.S.

A Stanford Grad, from San Antonio, TX. Gabriella is passionate about Technology and Education. She began her professional career as an Educator, receiving her Bachelors in English and Communication Studies from Texas State University, and her Masters in Education from Stanford University. During the global pandemic, as the world changed, Gabriella decided to change with it and try something new! Passionate about using technology to change the world (especially within the Education sphere), she began learning to code with SheCodes, an organization dedicated to teaching women how to code in order to close the gender gap in the tech industry. She is now a Front-End Developer who is proficient with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, using APIs, & React, and can make websites that are responsive & look good on any device, including mobile phones. In addition to being a skilled web developer, Gabriella knows how to communicate, execute, build relationships, & collaborate well with others, and she is passionate about making the world a better place. In her free time, she enjoys traveling, hiking, going to the ocean, and being in nature.

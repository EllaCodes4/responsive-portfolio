Hi I'm Gabriella Corales

Front-End Developer, based in the U.S.

A Stanford Grad, from San Antonio, TX, once Educator, now Front End Developer! Gabriella is passionate about elevating people & under-served communities using education and technology. She attended Stanford University as a First Generation college student and went on to passionately teach at schools serving under-served communities for nearly a decade. During the pandemic, as the world changed, Gabriella decided to change with it. She started learning to code with SheCodes, an organization dedicated to teaching women how to code in order to close the gender gap in tech. She is now a Front-End Developer who knows HTML, CSS, and JavaScript (React*) and has experience with other technologies including API Integration, Responsive Web Development, Git, Git Hub, Netlify, etc. Gabriella has always been in the business of breaking barriers, and she has no intention of stopping now! She is passionate about making the world a better, more equitable place, and is joining the tech industry with the same passion for elevating people & under-served communities using education and technology. In her free time, she enjoys hiking, traveling, going to the ocean, and being in nature.
